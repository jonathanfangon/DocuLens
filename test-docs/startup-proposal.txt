EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

NovaByte Technologies is seeking $12M in Series A funding at a $48M pre-money valuation to scale our AI-powered supply chain optimization platform. Since our seed round in Q2 2025, we have grown ARR from $400K to $2.8M, secured 34 enterprise customers including three Fortune 500 companies (Unilever, Caterpillar, and Tyson Foods), and expanded our team from 6 to 28 employees.

Our platform uses machine learning to predict supply chain disruptions 14-21 days before they occur, enabling procurement teams to reroute orders, adjust inventory, and negotiate backup supplier contracts proactively. In controlled studies with our beta customers, NovaByte reduced supply chain downtime by 62% and saved an average of $1.4M per customer annually.

MARKET OPPORTUNITY

The global supply chain management market is valued at $19.3B in 2025 and projected to reach $37.4B by 2030 (CAGR 14.2%). The AI-specific segment is growing at 28.7% CAGR. Key market tailwinds include:

- Post-pandemic supply chain fragility driving demand for predictive tools
- Rising geopolitical tensions (US-China trade, Red Sea shipping disruptions) increasing supply chain complexity
- New SEC climate disclosure rules requiring companies to report Scope 3 emissions across their supply chains, creating demand for visibility tools
- Legacy ERP systems (SAP, Oracle) struggling to provide real-time predictive capabilities

COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

Direct competitors include Resilinc ($85M raised), Everstream Analytics ($38M raised), and Interos ($110M raised). NovaByte differentiates on three axes:

1. SPEED TO VALUE: Our no-code integration connects to existing ERP systems in 48 hours vs. 3-6 months for competitors
2. PREDICTION ACCURACY: Our proprietary transformer model achieves 91.3% accuracy on 14-day disruption predictions vs. industry average of 67%
3. PRICING: $8,500/month base vs. $25,000+ for comparable solutions, enabling mid-market penetration
