EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of April 1, 2026, by and between:

EMPLOYER: CloudScale Solutions, Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal office at 2200 Mission Street, Suite 400, San Francisco, CA 94110 ("Company")

EMPLOYEE: Jordan Michael Rivera, residing at 1847 Dolores Street, Apt 3B, San Francisco, CA 94110 ("Employee")

RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Company desires to employ Employee as Senior Staff Software Engineer, and Employee desires to accept such employment, subject to the terms and conditions set forth herein.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

1. POSITION AND DUTIES

1.1 Title: Senior Staff Software Engineer, Platform Infrastructure Team
1.2 Reporting to: Maria Santos, VP of Engineering
1.3 Start Date: April 15, 2026
1.4 Location: Hybrid — minimum 3 days per week at San Francisco office
1.5 Employee shall devote full business time and attention to Company duties and shall not engage in outside employment without prior written consent.

2. COMPENSATION

2.1 Base Salary: $245,000 per year, paid semi-monthly
2.2 Signing Bonus: $35,000, payable within 30 days of start date. If Employee voluntarily terminates within 12 months, signing bonus must be repaid on a pro-rata basis.
2.3 Annual Bonus: Target of 20% of base salary ($49,000), based on individual and company performance metrics. Bonus is discretionary and not guaranteed.
2.4 Equity: 45,000 stock options at a strike price of $4.12 per share (current 409A valuation). Options vest over 4 years with a 1-year cliff (25% at 12 months, then monthly vesting). Subject to the terms of the 2024 Equity Incentive Plan.

3. BENEFITS

3.1 Health Insurance: Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage. Company pays 90% of premiums for Employee and 70% for dependents.
3.2 401(k): Company matches 50% of contributions up to 6% of salary.
3.3 PTO: 20 days paid vacation, 10 paid holidays, 5 sick days per year. Unused PTO carries over up to a maximum of 30 days.
3.4 Other: $2,000 annual learning & development budget, $500 home office stipend, commuter benefits.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY AND INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

4.1 Employee acknowledges they will have access to Confidential Information including trade secrets, source code, customer data, financial information, and business strategies.
4.2 Employee agrees not to disclose Confidential Information during or after employment, except as required to perform duties.
4.3 All inventions, works of authorship, and intellectual property created during employment and related to Company business shall be the sole property of the Company ("Work Product").
4.4 Employee assigns all rights, title, and interest in Work Product to the Company.
4.5 Employee represents that Exhibit A lists all prior inventions that Employee wishes to exclude from this Agreement.

5. NON-SOLICITATION

5.1 For a period of 12 months following termination, Employee shall not directly or indirectly solicit, recruit, or hire any employee or contractor of the Company.
5.2 For a period of 12 months following termination, Employee shall not solicit business from any customer or prospective customer with whom Employee had material contact during the last 12 months of employment.

6. TERMINATION

6.1 At-Will Employment: Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time, with or without cause, with or without notice.
6.2 Severance: If the Company terminates Employee without Cause (as defined in Section 6.4), and Employee executes a general release of claims, Employee shall receive:
    (a) 3 months of base salary continuation
    (b) 3 months of COBRA premium payments
    (c) 3 months accelerated vesting of unvested stock options
6.3 Change of Control: If Employee is terminated without Cause within 12 months of a Change of Control event, severance in Section 6.2 shall be doubled to 6 months.
6.4 "Cause" means: (i) material breach of this Agreement; (ii) conviction of a felony; (iii) willful misconduct causing material harm to the Company; (iv) continued failure to perform duties after written notice and 30-day cure period.

7. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

7.1 Any dispute arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in San Francisco, California, under the rules of JAMS.
7.2 The prevailing party shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorneys' fees.

8. GENERAL PROVISIONS

8.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations and agreements.
8.2 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
8.3 If any provision is found to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.
8.4 This Agreement may not be amended except in writing signed by both parties.
